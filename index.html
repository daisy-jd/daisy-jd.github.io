HSBC

Digital Innovation Internship

What does this internship involve?

You will learn about the technology that delivers customers and employees digital access to HSBC UK. You will learn about the importance of using innovation to solve business problems, while collaborating with teams across HSBC. The internship covers a range of areas including AI, Metaverse and Quantum Computing.

Who can apply?

  1. Penultimate year students
  2. Some experience of coding
  3. Interest in emerging technologies

When does the internship open?

17th September 2024

Morgan Stanley

Technology Summer Analyst Program

What does this internship involve?

The internship will provide an overview of Morgan Stanley's technology department. As an intern you will work on projects, network, complete team-building excercises and solve real-world business problems. Furthermore, you will gain a better understanding of Morgan Stanley as a company and gain greater financial knoweldge.

Who can apply?

  1. Penultimate year students from a Computer Science or STEM degree background
  2. Strong understanding of Operating Systems, familiar with foundations of at least one programming language
  3. Passion for technology and strong problem-solving skills

Nomura

Information Technology Summer Internship

What does this internship involve?

A 9-week internship where you will gain technical knowledge and training, gain hands on experience and gain an understanding of Nomura as a company. You will complete tasks similar to the level of a first-year analyst.

Who can apply?

  1. Penultimate year students graduating in 2026
  2. Fluent in English
  3. Available for full-time employment from July 2026
